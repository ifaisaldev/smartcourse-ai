About SmartCourse

An intelligent course recommendation system.

Technical Details

SmartCourse utilizes two distinct approaches to recommendation:

  • TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency): Analyzes the frequency of keywords in your query against course descriptions to find exact matches. Best for specific technical terms.
  • Neural Sentence Transformers: Uses a pre-trained deep learning model (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) to understand the meaning and context of your query, finding semantically similar courses even if they don't share exact keywords.

Dataset

The system is trained on a dataset of over 3,000 courses from various online platforms, covering topics in Business, Finance, Technology, and more.
